<p><p>The flame retardant polyester fibre market is categorized into three main types: Phosphorous Flame Retardant, Halogen Flame Retardant, and Other types. Phosphorous flame retardants incorporate phosphorus compounds that form a protective char layer when exposed to heat, enhancing fire resistance. Halogen flame retardants utilize bromine or chlorine compounds, effectively interrupting combustion processes but raising environmental concerns. Other market types encompass various innovative and emerging flame retardant technologies, including nitrogen-based additives and intumescent systems, offering diverse solutions for fire safety applications.</p></p>
