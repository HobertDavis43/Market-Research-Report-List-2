<p><p>The amphoteric surfactants market comprises various types including betaine, amine oxide, amphoacetates, amphopropionates, and sultaines. Betaine surfactants are known for their mildness and skin compatibility, making them popular in personal care products. Amine oxides serve as both surfactants and foam boosters. Amphoacetates exhibit excellent solubility and less irritation. Amphopropionates offer similar benefits with improved stability. Sultaines are recognized for their high frothing ability and compatibility with other surfactants. Together, these types cater to diverse applications in cosmetics, household cleaners, and industrial formulations.</p></p>
