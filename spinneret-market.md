<p><strong>The Spinneret Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Melt Spinning Spinneret</li><li>Wet Spinneret</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinneret market is divided into two main types based on the spinning process: melt spinning and wet spinning. Melt spinning involves extruding molten polymer through spinnerets to create fibers that cool and solidify rapidly, used primarily in synthetic fibers like nylon and polyester. In contrast, wet spinning entails dissolving polymers in a chemical solvent, then extruding through spinnerets into a coagulation bath, allowing fibers to form through precipitation, commonly used for materials like rayon. Both methods cater to different textile needs and applications.</p></p>
