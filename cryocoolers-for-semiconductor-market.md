<p><p>Cryocoolers for the semiconductor market include several types: </p><p>1. **GM (Gifford-McMahon) Cryocoolers** utilize a two-phase helium cycle for cooling, offering reliability and efficiency.</p><p>2. **PT (Pulse Tube) Cryocoolers** eliminate moving parts, reducing vibration while providing effective cooling through oscillating gas flow.</p><p>3. **JT (Joule-Thomson) Cryocoolers** leverage the Joule-Thomson effect for direct cooling with minimal complexity.</p><p>4. **Stirling Cryocoolers** use a cyclic compression and expansion process, ideal for low-temperature applications.</p><p>5. **Brayton Cryocoolers** operate on a closed regenerative cycle, suitable for high-power semiconductor applications.</p></p>
