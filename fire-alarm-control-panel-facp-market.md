<p><p>A Fire Alarm Control Panel (FACP) is a crucial component in fire protection systems, managing and monitoring alarm signals from various devices. The market for FACPs can be categorized into two main types: conventional and addressable. Conventional systems rely on zones to identify alarms, making them simpler and cost-effective for smaller applications. In contrast, addressable systems provide precise identification of specific devices, enhancing response time and diagnostic capabilities, making them suitable for larger, more complex facilities requiring advanced monitoring.</p></p>
