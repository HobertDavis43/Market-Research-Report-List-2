<p><p>The Membrane Oxygenator market is primarily divided into Flat Sheet Membrane Oxygenators and Hollow Fibre Membrane Oxygenators. Flat Sheet Membrane Oxygenators utilize flat membrane sheets to facilitate gas exchange, offering high surface area for efficient oxygenation. In contrast, Hollow Fibre Membrane Oxygenators feature numerous microscopic fibers that enhance blood surface contact, improving gas transfer efficiency. Both types are crucial in medical applications, particularly during cardiac surgeries and various respiratory support procedures, ensuring effective oxygen delivery and carbon dioxide removal.</p></p>
