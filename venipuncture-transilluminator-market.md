<p><p>The venipuncture transilluminator market is segmented into two main types: infrared and infrared fluorescence. Infrared transilluminators use infrared light to illuminate veins, enhancing visibility for venipuncture procedures. This method is particularly useful in patients with challenging venous access. In contrast, infrared fluorescence transilluminators employ a combination of infrared light and fluorescent markers, providing even more precise visualization of veins by highlighting them with enhanced contrast. Both types improve the efficiency and accuracy of venipuncture procedures in clinical settings.</p></p>
