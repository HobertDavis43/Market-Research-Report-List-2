<p><p>The automated labeling machine market is categorized into three primary types. Pressure sensitive/self-adhesive labelers use pre-adhesive labels that stick upon contact, ideal for various surfaces. Stretch sleeve/shrink-sleeve labelers apply flexible labels that conform to container shapes, providing a seamless look and enhanced branding. Glue-based labelers utilize adhesive to bond labels onto products, suitable for heavier applications. Each type serves distinct labeling needs across industries, impacting efficiency, product presentation, and consumer engagement.</p></p>
