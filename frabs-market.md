<p><p>FRABS (Flame Resistant and Anti-Burning Systems) market types are categorized into non-halogen and halogen types based on their chemical composition. Non-halogen FRABS utilize alternative flame-retardant materials that are less harmful to the environment and human health, appealing to eco-conscious industries. In contrast, halogen FRABS contain halogen compounds, providing effective fire resistance but potentially releasing toxic emissions when burned. Each type has distinct applications in industries requiring fire safety, such as construction, transportation, and electronics, balancing performance and environmental considerations.</p></p>
