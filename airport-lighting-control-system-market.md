<p><p>The Airport Lighting Control System market encompasses various types of lighting solutions essential for safe aircraft operations. Approach lights guide aircraft during landing, enhancing visibility in adverse conditions. Runway lights delineate the runway for takeoffs and landings. Taxiway lights facilitate safe movement of aircraft on the ground, while apron lights illuminate the parking areas. Stop bars signal when aircraft must halt before entering runways. Additional systems include lighting for hangars and maintenance areas, ensuring comprehensive safety and operational efficiency at airports.</p></p>
