<p><p>The brake system market encompasses several types, each utilizing different mechanisms for operation. Mechanical brakes rely on physical components like levers and cables for activation. Hydraulic brakes use fluid pressure to transmit force, providing effective stopping power. Pneumatic brakes operate on compressed air principles, commonly found in heavy vehicles. Electromagnetic brakes utilize magnetic fields for control, offering precise stopping. Combined systems integrate multiple technologies for enhanced performance, ensuring safety and efficiency across various vehicle applications.</p></p>
