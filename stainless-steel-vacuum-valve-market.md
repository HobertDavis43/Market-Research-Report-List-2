<p><p>The stainless steel vacuum valve market is categorized into two main types: manual and pneumatic valves. Manual valves require physical operation for control, offering simplicity and reliability, making them suitable for smaller systems. In contrast, pneumatic valves utilize compressed air for actuation, allowing for remote operation and automated processes in larger systems. Each type serves distinct applications across industries like food processing, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing, balancing efficiency, safety, and operational needs in vacuum environments.</p></p>
