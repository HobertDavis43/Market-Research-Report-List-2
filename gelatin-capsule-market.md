<p><strong>The Gelatin Capsule Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stomach-soluble Gelatin Capsules</li><li>Enterosoluble Vacant Gelatin Capsules</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gelatin capsule market is primarily segmented into three types: stomach-soluble gelatin capsules, enterosoluble vacant gelatin capsules, and other types. Stomach-soluble gelatin capsules dissolve in the acidic environment of the stomach, releasing their contents quickly. Enterosoluble capsules, on the other hand, are designed to dissolve in the intestines, protecting sensitive ingredients from stomach acid. Other market types may include specialized formulations for controlled release or specific delivery mechanisms, catering to niche pharmaceutical and dietary supplement needs.</p></p>
