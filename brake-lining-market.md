<p><p>The brake lining market comprises three main types: Asbestos Type, Semimetal Type, and NAO (Non-Asbestos Organic) Type. Asbestos Type linings offer high durability and thermal resistance but have declined due to health concerns. Semimetal Type linings combine metals like copper and steel for improved performance and longevity, making them popular in heavy-duty applications. NAO Type linings, made from organic materials, provide quiet operation and reduced dust emissions, prioritizing environmental safety and efficiency in passenger vehicles. Each type caters to specific performance and safety requirements.</p></p>
