ADSL (Asymmetric Digital Subscriber Line) offers broadband over existing copper phone lines, while VDSL (Very High Bitrate Digital Subscriber Line) provides faster speeds over shorter distances. Ethernet supports high-speed local area networking, ideal for connecting devices. GPON (Gigabit Passive Optical Network) delivers high-speed fiber-optic internet to homes, enabling greater bandwidth. Other emerging markets may include wireless technologies and integrated systems that cater to evolving consumer demands for connectivity and smart home functionalities.</p></p>
