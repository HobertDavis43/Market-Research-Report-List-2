<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Rheolytic Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Mechanical Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Aspiration Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical thrombectomy devices market includes various types designed to remove blood clots and restore blood flow. Ultrasonic thrombectomy devices utilize high-frequency sound waves to fragment clots for easier removal. Rheolytic thrombectomy devices employ a combination of mechanical forces and saline solutions to dissolve clots. Mechanical thrombectomy devices use specialized catheters to physically extract clots. Aspiration thrombectomy devices apply suction to aspirate clots. Other specialized technologies or hybrid approaches may also be included in this diverse market landscape.</p></p>
