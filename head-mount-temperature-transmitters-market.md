<p><p>Head-mount temperature transmitters are devices used for precise temperature measurement and transmission in industrial applications. The market is primarily segmented into thermocouples and Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs). Thermocouples consist of two dissimilar metals that generate a voltage correlated to temperature differences, providing wide temperature ranges and fast response times. RTDs use the resistance change in metal with temperature, offering high accuracy and stability, particularly in moderate temperature ranges. Both types are essential for various process control and monitoring applications.</p></p>
