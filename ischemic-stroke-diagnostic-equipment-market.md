<p><p>The ischemic stroke diagnostic equipment market encompasses various types including thrombotic and embolic strokes. Thrombotic strokes occur when a blood clot forms in an artery supplying blood to the brain, typically caused by plaque buildup. Embolic strokes happen when a blood clot or debris from another part of the body travels to the brain, blocking a vessel. Other types may include cryptogenic strokes, where no clear cause is identified. Diagnostic equipment aids in timely identification and treatment of these conditions.</p></p>
