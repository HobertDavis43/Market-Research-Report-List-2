<p><p>The dyestuff and pigments market encompasses various types of colorants used in textiles, plastics, and other materials. Disperse dyes are water-insoluble and used for synthetic fibers. Reactive dyes form covalent bonds with substrates, ideal for cotton. Sulfur dyes are cost-effective for dark colors and used on cellulosic fibers. Vat dyes offer excellent lightfastness and are used on cotton. Acid dyes are versatile for protein and nylon fibers. Pigments are insoluble colorants, used in coatings, plastics, and cosmetics, while other dyes include specific formulations for niche applications.</p></p>
