<p><strong>The Polyurethane-based Liquid-Applied Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane-based liquid-applied membranes are categorized into two main types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component membranes are ready-to-use products that cure through moisture in the air, making them easier to apply and less labor-intensive. In contrast, two-component membranes consist of a base and a hardener that must be mixed prior to application, offering superior performance, strength, and durability. Both types are utilized in waterproofing applications across construction, roofing, and infrastructure projects, catering to varying needs and conditions.</p></p>
