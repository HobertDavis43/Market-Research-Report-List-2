<p><strong>The Switchable Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic</li><li>Thermochromic</li><li>SPD</li><li>PDLC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The switchable glass market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Electrochromic**: Changes tint or opacity when an electrical voltage is applied, providing shading and privacy control. </p><p>2. **Thermochromic**: Alters its transparency based on temperature fluctuations, responding to heat levels. </p><p>3. **Suspended Particle Device (SPD)**: Utilizes suspended particles to control light transmission, allowing for adjustable tinting. </p><p>4. **Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC)**: Transforms from opaque to transparent when voltage is applied, offering enhanced privacy and light control.</p></p>
