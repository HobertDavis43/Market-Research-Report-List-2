<p><p>The seawater reverse osmosis desalination pump market is primarily categorized into two types: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move seawater through membranes, providing high flow rates and efficiency for large-scale operations. In contrast, positive displacement pumps move a fixed volume of water per cycle, generating high pressure, making them suitable for applications requiring precise flow control and efficiency at lower flow rates. Both types play critical roles in enhancing desalination processes.</p></p>
