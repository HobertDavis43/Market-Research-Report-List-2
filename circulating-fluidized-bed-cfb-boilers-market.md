<p><p>Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB) boilers are categorized into three market types based on operating pressure and temperature: Subcritical, Supercritical, and Ultra-Supercritical. Subcritical CFB boilers operate below the critical point, offering lower efficiency and moderate emissions. Supercritical CFB boilers exceed critical pressure, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions significantly. Ultra-Supercritical CFB boilers operate at even higher temperatures and pressures, maximizing thermal efficiency and minimizing environmental impact, making them the most advanced and environmentally friendly option for coal combustion.</p></p>
