<p><p>The disposable laparoscope market includes various instruments designed for minimally invasive surgeries. Bipolar instruments use simultaneous electrical currents for coagulation, reducing thermal spread. Monopolar instruments utilize a single electrode for cutting and coagulation, ensuring precise tissue interaction. Trocars serve as access points for surgical instruments, enabling insertion into the body. Graspers secure tissues during procedures, while dissectors help separate and manipulate tissues for better visibility and access. Together, these instruments streamline surgical processes, enhancing patient safety and recovery.</p></p>
