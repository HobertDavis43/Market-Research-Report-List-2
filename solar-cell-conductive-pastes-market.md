<p><p>The solar cell conductive pastes market is classified into three main types: front side silver pastes, back side silver pastes, and back side aluminum pastes. Front side silver pastes are primarily used for creating electrical contacts on the sun-facing side of solar cells, enhancing efficiency. Back side silver pastes serve a similar purpose on the rear side, improving conductivity and overall performance. Back side aluminum pastes, on the other hand, are used to form a reflective layer that aids in light absorption, boosting energy generation.</p></p>
