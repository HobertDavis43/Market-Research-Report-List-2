<p><p>The turbomachinery market encompasses various types, each serving distinct functions. Pumps are used to transport liquids, while compressors increase the pressure of gases. Turbines convert fluid energy into mechanical work, commonly seen in power generation and propulsion. Motors and generators facilitate the conversion between electrical and mechanical energy. Fans move air or gases for cooling and ventilation. Engines, typically internal combustion or gas turbines, drive vehicles or machinery. The "Others" category includes specialized turbomachinery applications across various industries.</p></p>
