<p><p>Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) services encompass various market types, including Targeted Sequencing, Whole Exome Sequencing (WES), and Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS). Targeted Sequencing focuses on specific genes of interest, enabling precise analysis of genetic variations related to diseases. Whole Exome Sequencing examines the coding regions of the genome, providing insights into protein-coding variations. Whole Genome Sequencing, the most comprehensive option, analyzes the entire genome, offering a complete picture of an organism's genetic information, applicable in various fields including research and clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
