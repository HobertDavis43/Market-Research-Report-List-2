<p><p>The hormonal contraceptive implant market primarily consists of two types: Levonorgestrel contraceptive implants and Etonogestrel contraceptive implants. Levonorgestrel implants, such as Norplant, release the hormone levonorgestrel to prevent ovulation and thicken cervical mucus, providing long-term contraception. Etonogestrel implants, like Nexplanon, deliver etonogestrel, offering similar benefits but with different formulation and duration. Both types provide effective, reversible contraception options that are convenient and easy to use, appealing to women seeking long-term birth control solutions.</p></p>
